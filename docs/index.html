Welcome to China Dragon

Serving Moulton's Chinese cuisine for over 25 years

Our Story

Since 1999, China Dragon has been proudly owned and operated by the Huang family. We truly appreciate everyone who has supported us over the years and helped us grow into a local favorite in Lawrence County.

Our Community

Proudly serving Moulton, Alabama, China Dragon has been part of the community for over two decades. We're grateful to serve neighbors, friends, and families who have supported us year after year.
