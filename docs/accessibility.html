Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

Our Commitment to Accessibility

China Dragon Restaurant is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all of our users.

Website Accessibility

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our website includes the following accessibility features:

Navigation and Structure

  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements can be accessed using a keyboard
  • Skip Links: "Skip to main content" links are provided on every page
  • Semantic HTML: Proper heading hierarchy and semantic elements for screen readers
  • ARIA Labels: Descriptive labels for interactive elements and navigation

Visual Design

  • Color Contrast: Text and background colors meet WCAG AA contrast requirements
  • Responsive Design: Website adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels
  • Clear Typography: Readable fonts with appropriate sizing
  • Focus Indicators: Visible focus states for keyboard navigation

Content

  • Alternative Text: Images include descriptive alt text
  • Clear Language: Content written in plain, easy-to-understand language
  • Consistent Layout: Predictable navigation and page structure

Restaurant Accessibility

Our physical restaurant location is designed to be accessible to all guests:

Physical Access

  • Wheelchair Accessible Entrance: Level entry with no steps
  • Accessible Parking: Designated accessible parking spaces available
  • Wheelchair Accessible Seating: Tables with appropriate clearance
  • Accessible Restrooms: ADA-compliant restroom facilities
  • Wide Aisles: Clear pathways throughout the restaurant

Service Accommodations

  • Menu Assistance: Staff available to read menu items and answer questions
  • Dietary Accommodations: We work with guests to accommodate food allergies and dietary restrictions
  • Takeout Service: Curbside pickup available upon request
  • Patient Service: Our staff is trained to provide patient, courteous service to all guests

Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to be compatible with:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Browser zoom functionality

Third-Party Content

Some content on our website may be provided by third parties (such as Google Maps). While we strive to ensure all content is accessible, we cannot always control the accessibility of third-party content.

Ongoing Efforts

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website and restaurant. Our efforts include:

  • Regular accessibility audits and testing
  • Staff training on accessibility and inclusive service
  • Gathering feedback from users with disabilities
  • Implementing improvements based on user feedback
  • Staying current with accessibility best practices and standards

Technical Specifications

Our website relies on the following technologies to work with your web browser and assistive technologies:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript (for enhanced functionality, but not required for basic access)
  • ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications)

Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible. We are working to address these limitations. If you have difficulty accessing any content, please contact us and we will:

  • Provide the information in an alternative format
  • Assist you over the phone
  • Work with you to find a suitable solution
